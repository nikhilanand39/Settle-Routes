Our History

Founded in the vibrant city of Chandigarh, India, Settleroutes began with a clear vision: to bridge the gap between global opportunities and aspiring individuals. What started as a dedicated consultancy has rapidly grown into a comprehensive service provider, assisting countless clients in navigating the complexities of international visas and travel. Our journey is marked by a legacy of successful applications, delighted travelers, and a steadfast commitment to turning international dreams into reality. We continuously evolve, adapting to global changes, while maintaining our core values that put our clients first.
